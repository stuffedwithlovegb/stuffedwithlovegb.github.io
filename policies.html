Booking Information & Event Policies

We believe planning your event should be simple and stress-free! The information below explains our booking process, payment schedule, travel details, and event policies so you know exactly what to expect. If you ever have questions, we are always happy to help.

Booking Your Event

Reservation Deposit

A non-refundable $100 deposit is required to reserve your event date and time. Event dates are not guaranteed until the deposit has been received and your booking has been confirmed by Stuffed With Love.

Final Payment

The remaining balance is due on or before the event unless other arrangements have been made in advance.

Corporate, school, daycare, nonprofit, and community organizations may be invoiced according to the payment terms agreed upon during the booking process.

We accept major credit cards and other approved electronic payment methods.

Guest Count

Final guest counts must be provided at least 7 days before the event. Your selected package reserves the included number of participants.

Additional participants may be accommodated based on supply and staffing availability and will be billed according to your package pricing.

Please notify us as early as possible if your guest count changes so we can prepare enough plushes, hearts, certificates, take-home bags, and supplies.

Changes & Cancellations

Cancellation Policy

All reservation deposits are non-refundable.

Events canceled more than 14 days before the scheduled event may transfer their deposit to one new event date within 6 months, subject to availability.

Events canceled within 14 days of the scheduled event will forfeit their deposit. Custom, personalized, or special-order items already purchased or created for the event may also be non-refundable.

Rescheduling Policy

One complimentary reschedule is permitted when requested at least 14 days before the original event date.

The new event date must be selected within 6 months and is subject to availability. Additional rescheduling requests may be subject to a fee.

Unforeseen Circumstances

In the rare event that circumstances beyond our reasonable control prevent Stuffed With Love from providing your event, we will work with you to reschedule or provide an appropriate solution.

These circumstances may include severe weather, unsafe travel conditions, power outages, government restrictions, equipment failure, illness, emergencies, or other unexpected situations.

Travel & Event Day

Travel Policy

Travel within 15 miles of Green Bay is included at no additional charge.

Events outside our standard service area may be subject to a travel fee. Any applicable travel charge will always be discussed and approved before your booking is finalized.

Arrival & Setup

Stuffed With Love typically arrives approximately 30–45 minutes before the scheduled event start time to complete setup.

Larger events, custom installations, or events with additional experience stations may require more setup time.

Hosts must ensure that the designated setup area is clean, dry, accessible, and available when our team arrives.

Event Space Requirements

Our full Stuffed With Love experience fits comfortably in an area about the size of one standard parking space.

We can set up indoors or outdoors and are happy to customize our layout for smaller spaces whenever possible. The setup area must be level, clean, dry, accessible, and free from hazards.

Stuffed With Love brings its own portable power source, so an electrical outlet is not typically required.

Event Start Time

Events will begin at the scheduled time whenever possible.

If guests or the host are delayed, the event may still need to end at the originally scheduled time so we can honor other bookings and staffing commitments.

Weather Policy

Outdoor events require a safe weather backup plan.

If rain, strong wind, extreme temperatures, storms, or other unsafe conditions are expected, we will work with you to move the event indoors or reschedule when possible.

A covered pavilion or tent may be acceptable depending on the weather, but it must fully protect our equipment, plushes, paperwork, activity stations, staff, and guests.

The safety of our guests, staff, supplies, and equipment will always be our top priority.

Guests & Safety

Adult Supervision

A responsible adult must remain present throughout the event. Stuffed With Love staff provide the plush-building experience but are not responsible for childcare or supervising children.

Parents, guardians, hosts, or event organizers remain responsible for guest behavior and supervision at all times.

Guest Safety

We work hard to provide a fun, organized, and safe experience. Guests must follow all instructions provided by Stuffed With Love staff while participating in the event.

Parents, guardians, and event hosts are responsible for determining whether the activities, plush accessories, and small items are appropriate for each participant.

Stuffed With Love is not responsible for injuries caused by unsafe behavior, failure to follow instructions, inadequate supervision, or conditions at the host's venue.

Food & Beverages

To help protect our stuffing machine, plushes, certificates, activity stations, and other supplies, we kindly ask that food and beverages remain separate from the Stuffed With Love setup whenever possible.

Hosts are responsible for informing Stuffed With Love of any known venue restrictions, allergy concerns, or special accommodations that may affect the event setup.

Personal Belongings

Stuffed With Love is not responsible for lost, stolen, damaged, or unattended personal belongings during an event.

Plushes, Supplies & Customization

Equipment & Supplies

We make every reasonable effort to provide the plush styles, clothing, accessories, and event features requested for your event.

Due to supplier availability, shipping delays, inventory changes, or discontinued products, Stuffed With Love reserves the right to substitute plush animals, clothing, accessories, or materials with comparable options of equal or greater value when necessary.

Plush Selection

Plush options are subject to availability and may vary by package, season, event size, and supplier inventory.

Unless a specific plush selection has been confirmed in writing, Stuffed With Love may provide an assortment of available plush friends for guests to choose from.

Custom Items & Themes

We love helping bring your vision to life! Custom vinyl designs, themed items, personalized apparel, specialty plushes, branded accessories, and other special requests require a minimum of 14 days' notice.

Larger custom orders, corporate branding, or specialty products may require additional production time.

While we will make every reasonable effort to accommodate custom requests, availability, design complexity, materials, supplier inventory, and production timelines may limit what we can provide.

Stuffed With Love reserves the right to decline or modify a custom request when necessary, but we will always communicate clearly and do our best to create something you love.

Personalized & Special-Order Items

Personalized, branded, custom-made, or special-order items cannot usually be reused for another event and may be non-refundable once production or purchasing has begun.

Photography & Promotion

Photography

We love capturing special moments from our events. With permission, photos or videos taken during an event may be used on the Stuffed With Love website, social media pages, promotional materials, and business listings.

If the host, parent, guardian, venue, school, company, or organization prefers that certain guests not be photographed, please let us know before the event. We will gladly honor those requests.

For schools, corporate events, public events, and organizations with their own media policies, Stuffed With Love will follow the photography guidelines provided by the event organizer.

Questions About Our Policies?

We want you to feel confident and excited about your event. If you have any questions about these policies, your venue, guest count, setup, payment terms, or special requests, please contact us before booking.

We are always happy to help make your Stuffed With Love experience as smooth and memorable as possible.
