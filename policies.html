Booking & Event Policies

Please review the information below before reserving your event.

Reservation Deposit

A non-refundable $100 deposit is required to reserve your event date and time. Event dates are not guaranteed until the deposit has been received and confirmed.

Final Payment

The remaining balance is due on the day of the event before the party begins. We accept cash, Venmo, PayPal, and major credit cards.

Guest Count

Final guest counts must be provided at least 7 days prior to the event. Additional guests may be accommodated based on availability. Additional plush participants are $25 per child.

Cancellation Policy

All deposits are non-refundable. Events canceled more than 14 days before the scheduled date may transfer their deposit to a new event date within 6 months, subject to availability. Events canceled within 14 days of the scheduled date will forfeit their deposit.

Rescheduling Policy

One complimentary reschedule is permitted when requested at least 14 days before the event date. Additional reschedule requests may be subject to a fee.

Travel Policy

Travel within 15 miles of Green Bay is included at no additional charge. Events located beyond 15 miles may be subject to an additional travel fee. Most locations within 60 miles can be accommodated for an additional $20 travel fee.

Arrival & Setup

Stuffed With Love typically arrives 30–45 minutes before the scheduled event time to complete setup. Hosts should ensure the designated setup area is available and accessible upon arrival.

Equipment & Supplies

We strive to provide the plush styles, clothing, and accessories requested for your event. Due to availability and inventory changes, Stuffed With Love reserves the right to substitute plush animals, clothing, or accessories with comparable options of equal value when necessary.

Party Space Requirements

Hosts must provide adequate indoor space for guests, equipment, and event activities. The setup area should be clean, dry, and accessible upon arrival.

Adult Supervision

A responsible adult must remain present throughout the duration of the event. Stuffed With Love staff are not responsible for supervising children.

Weather Policy

Outdoor events are weather permitting. In the event of severe weather or unsafe conditions, events may be moved indoors or rescheduled.

Photography

Stuffed With Love may take photographs during events for promotional and marketing purposes. Parents or guardians may request that their child not be photographed, and we will gladly honor those requests.
