Questions? We've Got You Covered!

Everything you need to know before booking your Stuffed With Love experience.

More than entertainment, it is an unforgettable interactive experience where every guest creates memories they will treasure long after the event is over.

We transform your space into a charming little world where children become the creators of their very own plush friends. From choosing a plush and bringing it to life at our stuffing station to making a special wish during our signature heart ceremony, every step is designed to feel magical.

Guests can explore our interactive felt activity wall, visit the Friend Hotel, snap photos in our themed photo area, and, with select packages, browse our adorable boutique for extra accessories. Every child leaves with a personalized plush, special keepsakes, and wonderful memories.

Our experiences are most popular with children ages 3–12, but guests of all ages can enjoy creating their own plush friend. Younger children may need a little help from a parent or guardian, while older children and adults often enjoy the experience just as much.

Every Stuffed With Love package includes everything needed to create a magical plush-building experience. Guests receive a premium plush, stuffing, a heart insert, our signature heart ceremony, a birth certificate, and a take-home bag.

We also provide professional setup, friendly on-site staff, and interactive experience stations, including our stuffing station, Friend Hotel, themed photo area, and felt activity wall. Select packages may also include our plush boutique, accessories, outfits, and other premium add-ons.
