Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your Stuffed With Love event.

What exactly is a Stuffed With Love party?

More than a party, it's an unforgettable interactive experience. We transform your space into a charming little world where every child becomes the creator of their very own furry friend. From choosing a plush and bringing it to life at our stuffing station to making a special wish during our heart ceremony, every step is designed to feel magical. Kids can explore our interactive felt activity wall, visit the Friend Hotel, snap photos in our themed photo area, and, with select packages, shop our adorable boutique for extra accessories. Every guest leaves with a personalized plush, keepsakes, and memories they'll treasure long after the party ends.

What ages is this best for?

Our events are most popular for children ages 3–12, but kids of all ages and plenty of adults enjoy creating their own stuffed animals.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes. A deposit is required to reserve your event date and secure your booking. Deposit amounts, payment schedules, and cancellation information can be found on our Policy Page. If you have any questions about payment or booking, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How much space do you need?

We can set up indoors or outdoors and are happy to work with your space! Our full Stuffed With Love experience—including the stuffing station, interactive activity area, Friend Hotel, themed photo spot, and optional boutique—fits comfortably in an area about the size of one standard parking space. Don't have that much room? No problem! We can customize our setup to fit smaller spaces while still creating a magical experience for your guests. Even better: We bring our own power source, so no electrical outlet is required. Not sure if your space will work? Simply send us a photo of your event space, and we'll help design the perfect setup!.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for weekends and holiday events. However, feel free to contact us even if your event is coming up soon.

What's included?

Every Stuffed With Love package includes everything needed to create a magical plush-building experience! Guests receive a premium plush, stuffing, a heart ceremony, birth certificate, and a take-home bag. We also provide our professional setup, friendly on-site staff, and interactive experience stations—including our stuffing station, Friend Hotel, themed photo area, and activity stations. Select packages also include our plush boutique and additional premium add-ons.

Can you attend schools, festivals, and community events?

Absolutely! We love participating in school functions, daycare events, fairs, festivals, fundraisers, corporate events, and community celebrations.

Let's Create Something Special Together

Stuffed With Love was created to bring families together through fun, hands-on experiences and memories that last. We'd love to be part of your next celebration.
