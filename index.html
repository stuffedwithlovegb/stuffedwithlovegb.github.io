More Than a Plush Party

Stuffed With Love brings a complete mobile plush-building experience directly to your event anywhere in Northeast Wisconsin. We transform your space into a charming little world where guests can create, play, explore, and bring their very own plush friend to life.

Each guest begins by choosing a plush friend, then visits our stuffing station to help bring it to life. They add a special heart, make a wish during our signature heart ceremony, name their new friend, and complete a personalized birth certificate before taking their keepsake home.

The experience goes far beyond the stuffing station. Depending on the selected package, guests can explore our interactive felt activity wall, visit the Friend Hotel, pose for photos in our themed photo area, and shop our plush boutique for clothing and accessories. Together, these create the feeling of a tiny village made just for new furry friends.

Stuffed With Love is perfect for birthday celebrations, schools, daycares, company family events, holiday gatherings, festivals, fundraisers, and community events. We bring the equipment, supplies, décor, activities, setup, staffing, and cleanup so you can relax and enjoy the experience with your guests.
