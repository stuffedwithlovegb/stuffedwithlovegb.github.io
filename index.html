What We Do

Stuffed with Love is a mobile plush experience that brings everything to you. We arrive with our stuffing machine, pre-made plush animals, and all the materials needed to create a complete hands-on experience wherever we are set up.

Families and children choose their plush, help with the stuffing process, and watch it come to life right in front of them. It turns something simple into a shared moment that feels personal and memorable.

Our goal is to make the experience easy, fun, and meaningful from start to finish without any stress or rushing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your Stuffed With Love event.

🧸 What exactly is a Stuffed With Love party?

We bring our plush stuffing machine, stuffing materials, and plush animals directly to your location. Each child chooses an animal, helps bring it to life, and takes home a keepsake they'll treasure.

🎂 What ages is this best for?

Our events are most popular for children ages 3–12, but kids of all ages (and plenty of adults!) enjoy creating their own stuffed animals.

📍 What areas do you serve?

We're based in Green Bay and proudly serve Northeast Wisconsin, including De Pere, Ashwaubenon, Bellevue, Howard, Suamico, Appleton, and surrounding communities.

👧 How many children can participate?

Each package includes a set number of stuffed animals. If you're expecting more guests, let us know and we'll help build a package that fits your event.

🏡 How much space do you need?

We can set up indoors or outdoors. A small area for our stuffing machine and guest participation is usually all we need.

📅 How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for weekends and holiday events. However, feel free to contact us even if your event is coming up soon.

💚 What is included?

Every package includes plush animals, stuffing, heart inserts, birth certificates, setup, and the complete stuffing experience brought directly to your event.

🎉 Can you attend schools, festivals, and community events?

Absolutely! We love participating in school functions, daycare events, fairs, festivals, fundraisers, corporate events, and community celebrations.
