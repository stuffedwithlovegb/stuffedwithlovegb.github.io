What We Do

Stuffed With Love brings the complete stuff-a-plush experience directly to your location anywhere in Northeast Wisconsin. We provide the plush animals, stuffing equipment, accessories, and everything needed for an unforgettable hands-on activity.

Each guest chooses their own plush friend, helps bring it to life during the stuffing process, and takes home a keepsake they created themselves. It's more than a party favor—it's a memory they'll treasure long after the celebration ends.

Perfect for birthday parties, school events, daycares, community festivals, baby showers, holiday gatherings, and corporate family events. We handle the setup and experience so you can focus on enjoying the moment.
