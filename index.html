Who We Are

A family-owned business dedicated to creating magical moments through the art of plushie-making

Bringing Smiles Since 2018

At Cuddle Friends, we believe every child deserves a special companion. Our journey began in a small home studio with a simple mission: to create the softest, most lovable plushies that become lifelong friends.

Each plushie is designed with care, using only premium, child-safe materials. From playful pandas to cuddly unicorns, our collection is crafted to spark imagination and provide comfort for years to come.
