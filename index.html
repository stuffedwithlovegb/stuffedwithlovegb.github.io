What We Are

Stuffed With Love is a mobile plushie service inspired by the Build-A-Bear experience — but instead of going to a store, we bring everything to you. Kids and families design, customize, and create their own stuffed animals on the spot.

How It Works

Step 1

Choose Your Plush

Select from a variety of soft, high-quality stuffed animals.

Step 2

Customize It

Add outfits, scents, sounds, and personal touches to make it yours.

Step 3

We Come to You

We set up everything at your event, party, school, or home.

Why People Love It

We turn stuffed animals into memories. Every plush is unique because it’s created by the person who owns it. It’s not just a toy — it’s an experience.
