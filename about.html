Meet Our Family

A Green Bay family turning kids' parties into unforgettable memories

Creating Joy, Reducing Waste 💚

We're a local Green Bay family on a mission to make kids' parties more meaningful. As parents ourselves, we noticed something troubling: most party favors are cheap plastic that ends up in the trash within a week.

That's why we created Stuffed with Love! Instead of forgettable plastic toys, kids at our events get to create their very own stuffed animal friend—a cuddly companion they'll treasure for years.

🌍 Every stuffed animal created means less plastic waste in our beautiful Wisconsin community!
