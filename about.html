Meet Our Family

Creating Joy, Reducing Waste 💚

Creating Joy, Reducing Waste 💚

We’re a family in Green Bay creating a space where plush companions are brought to life in a simple, meaningful way.

Stuffed with Love was started by me, Tawny, my husband Forrest, and our kids Mason and Forrest Jr, with the idea of building something joyful and family-centered.

We provide the experience and the machine that allows children and families to take a pre-made plush and fill it themselves, turning it into something personal, memorable, and uniquely theirs.

Each visit is meant to feel special and unhurried. A chance to pick out a plush, take part in the stuffing process, and enjoy a small moment of excitement together.

Our hope is to give families a small memory they can take home, something soft to hold onto, and something that feels a little more personal because they helped create it.

We are proud to be a small business in Green Bay and deeply appreciate the support from our community as we grow and continue sharing this experience with local families.

🌍 Every stuffed animal means less plastic waste!
