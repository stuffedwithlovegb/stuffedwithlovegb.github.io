Creating Joy, One Memory at a Time 💛

Hi! We're Tawny and Forrest Hiatt, the family behind Stuffed With Love.

Our story began on Tawny's 27th birthday in Boise, Idaho, where we met through a mutual friend. As lifelong Packers fans, we instantly connected, and after our first trip to Green Bay in 2019, we fell in love with the community and knew it was where we wanted to build our future.

That dream became reality when we moved to Green Bay in 2022, bought our first home, got married, and welcomed our two amazing children, Mason and Forrest Jr.

Stuffed With Love was inspired by the things we love most: family, making memories, and of course, our fur babies. Our dogs, Lambeau and Link, and our kitties, Simba and Nala remind us every day of the comfort, companionship, and unconditional love that animals bring into our lives.

We created Stuffed With Love to give families a fun, hands-on experience that creates more than just a stuffed animal—it creates a special memory. Whether you're celebrating a birthday party, baby shower, school event, holiday gathering, or community celebration, we're honored to be part of those moments and help bring a little extra love to every occasion.

As a locally owned family business, we are proud to serve Green Bay and the surrounding communities. Our goal is simple: create joyful experiences, lasting memories, and smiles that families will remember long after the event is over.

From our family to yours, thank you for supporting our small business. We can't wait to celebrate with you. 💛

Let's Create Something Special Together

Stuffed With Love was created to bring families together through fun, hands-on experiences and memories that last. We'd love to be part of your next celebration.
